SQL for Support Analytics

Uncover ticket volume, SLA performance, and recurring issues. Provide Product teams with evidence.

-- Top issue categories, last 6 months
SELECT category, COUNT(*) as tickets,
AVG(resolution_hours) as avg_resolution
FROM support_tickets
WHERE created_at >= DATE('now','-6 months')
GROUP BY category ORDER BY tickets DESC;
